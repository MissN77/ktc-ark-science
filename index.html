Welcome to Ark Science Mastery

Everything you need to plan and deliver Science Mastery lessons, all in one place. Here's how to get started.

1
Pick your year and unit
Go to the Lessons tab. Select your year group and filter by subject. Click a unit to open it.
2
Read the Unit Guidance first
Inside each unit, open Unit Guidance to find the planning proforma and preparation booklet. This tells you the sequencing, prior knowledge, and common misconceptions.
3
Follow the lesson flow
Each lesson follows the same structure: Do Now (retrieval) > I Do (teacher input) > We Do (guided practice) > You Do (independent work) > Exit Ticket (check understanding). The coloured bars show you exactly how many slides are in each phase.
4
Preview or download
Every file has two buttons: Preview opens it in your browser (no download needed) and Download saves it to your computer. The main lesson PowerPoint is highlighted at the top of each lesson.
5
Use the other tabs
Assessments has diagnostic tests per year group. Revision has flashcards, required practical checklists, and command word practice for KS4. Curriculum has the long-term plan, curriculum map, and teacher guide.
Lesson structure at a glance
Do Now > I Do > We Do > You Do > Exit Ticket
Every lesson PowerPoint follows this sequence. The Do Now retrieves prior knowledge. I Do is your teacher input. We Do is guided practice together. You Do is independent work (worksheets, activities). The Exit Ticket checks if it stuck.
